Competitive Analysis

UBA

United Bank for Africa (UBA) PLC is a leading pan-African financial services institution with a global footprint. United Bank for Africa (UBA) is Africa’s Global bank with a rich history spanning over seven decades. The bank which is headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, is one of the most recognised financial institutions to originate from Sub-Saharan Africa with thriving operations in 20 African countries.

UBA also operates in the United Kingdom and France and we are the only sub Saharan African bank with a deposit taking license in the USA. We officially opened for business in Dubai, the United Arab Emirates in 2022.

The Challange

The United Bank for Africa (UBA) mobile application presents a semi-stable User Interface (UI) and an overall User Experience (UX) that could benefit from further improvement.

Users have been voicing strong dissatisfaction with the UBA mobile app, criticizing its design for not meeting expectations in terms of functionality, aesthetics, and ease of use. Many feel that the app lacks the polish and intuitiveness typically expected from a modern banking application. Key areas of concern include a cluttered layout, inconsistent button placements, and a color scheme that does not aid in smooth navigation or visual appeal.

The Approach

For the UBA challenge, I tackled issues with UI stability and UX flow to enhance usability. My approach focused on user research to identify pain points, followed by a redesign to streamline navigation, improve consistency, and boost app performance. The result? A smoother, more intuitive mobile banking experience that aligns with modern user expectations."
These painpoints included but were not limited to:

01. Lack of Visual appeal on user interface.

02. Insufficient user Experience.

03. Usability challenges.

04. Slow screen load time.
